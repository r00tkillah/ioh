IoH

The Inetnet of Hats (IoH) is an application of the Internet of Things (IoH) to hats. Specifically, to be used as badges for Portland B-Sides. Although the term IoT means many things to many people, effectively making it mean almost nothing to all the people, IoH is specific. In this context, we mean hats that are connected via 6lowpan.
